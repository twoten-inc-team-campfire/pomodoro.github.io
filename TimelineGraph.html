Class: TimelineGraph

TimelineGraph(date, timerSessions, maxGap) → {JSX.Element}

new TimelineGraph(date, timerSessions, maxGap) → {JSX.Element}

Displays a visual summary of a day's TimerSessions in the form of a marked timeline.
Parameters:
Name Type Description
date Date The date we want to model.
timerSessions Array.<TimerSession> The array of TimerSessions on that date. For now, we're assuming the array is well-formed. In other words, the TimerSessions are ordered sequentially and at least partially take place on the date in passed-in.
maxGap number The permitted gap between TimerSessions before we split them into separate sections, in minutes.
